Overview

BigDayTimer is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our Chrome extension. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use BigDayTimer.

üîí Privacy First Approach

We believe in minimal data collection. Most of your timer data never leaves your device, and we only collect what's absolutely necessary to provide our services.

What Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Timer Data: Event names, dates, times, and custom settings
  • Customizations: Background preferences, color choices, and display settings
  • Custom Images: Any personal images you upload for backgrounds (Free and Premium users)

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information only when necessary:

  • User ID: A randomly generated identifier to manage premium subscriptions
  • Purchase Information: Payment verification data from our payment processor (Paddle)
  • Error Reports: Anonymous crash reports and error logs to improve the extension
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous data about which features are used (opt-out available)

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, address) unless voluntarily provided for support
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Contents of your timer events or personal messages
  • Location data
  • Social media profiles or contacts

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage APIs. This means:

  • Your timer data stays on your device
  • We cannot access your personal timer information
  • Data is automatically backed up with your Chrome sync if enabled

Cloud Storage

We use minimal cloud storage only for:

  • Premium subscription verification (stored with Cloudflare KV)
  • Anonymous usage statistics for product improvement

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data
  • Premium Data: Retained as long as you have an active or valid premium license
  • Support Data: Deleted after 2 years or when no longer needed

Your Rights and Choices

You Have the Right To:

  • Access: Request information about data we store about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Portability: Export your local timer data
  • Opt-out: Disable anonymous usage statistics
  • Correction: Update any incorrect information

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  1. Email us at privacy@bigdaytimer.com
  2. Include your User ID (found in the extension settings)
  3. Specify which rights you'd like to exercise
  4. We'll respond within 30 days

Data Control Features:

Built into the extension:

  • Export all your timer data
  • Clear all local data
  • Disable analytics and crash reporting
  • Manage premium subscription
