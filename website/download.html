When will BigDayTimer be available?
We're targeting a March 2025 launch on the Chrome Web Store. Beta testing will begin in February 2025 for early access users.
Will it be free to use?
Yes! BigDayTimer offers a robust free version with all core features. Premium features are available for $4.99 (one-time purchase).
Which browsers are supported?
Initially Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera). Firefox and Safari support are planned for later in 2025.
How do I get notified of the release?
Click "Notify Me When Available" above to join our notification list. We'll email you as soon as BigDayTimer launches.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Your timer data is stored locally on your device. We only use minimal cloud storage for premium license verification.
Can I use it offline?
Yes! Once installed, BigDayTimer works completely offline. Internet is only needed for installation and premium feature verification.
