About BigDayTimer

Making every moment count towards your special day

Our Story

BigDayTimer was born from a simple idea: what if every time you opened your browser, you were reminded of the exciting event you're counting down to?

As a team of developers and designers who love celebrating life's big moments, we noticed that existing countdown apps were either too complicated, buried in phone notifications, or simply forgettable. We wanted something that would be right there with you as you browse, work, and live your digital life.

That's how BigDayTimer came to life - a beautiful, floating countdown timer that appears on every webpage, keeping your anticipation alive and making every browser session a little more exciting.
